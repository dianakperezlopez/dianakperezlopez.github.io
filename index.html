I am a Pre-Doctoral Fellow at the Yale Economics Department and Economic Growth Center, working with Professor Orazio Attanasio on Early Childhood Development. I previously worked as a research assistant at the Department of Economics at Princeton University and Universidad de los Andes, Colombia. I hold a B.A. and M.A. in Economics from Universidad de los Andes, Colombia. My interests include development, labor, and public economics (e.g., early childhood development, education, labor, and gender).
